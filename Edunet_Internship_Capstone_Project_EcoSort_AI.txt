Project Title:
EcoSort AI – Smart Waste Segregation using Deep Learning

Project Domain:
Artificial Intelligence | Computer Vision | Deep Learning | Sustainability | Green Skills

Project Description:
EcoSort AI is an AI-powered smart waste segregation system designed to promote proper waste management and environmental sustainability. The system allows users to upload an image of a waste item. Using Computer Vision and Deep Learning, the application identifies the type of waste, classifies it into one of four categories: Organic, Recyclable, Hazardous, and E-waste, and provides appropriate disposal guidance and sustainability information. The system also maintains prediction logs and analytics to encourage responsible waste disposal and environmental awareness.

Project Objectives:
• Detect waste from uploaded images using Artificial Intelligence.
• Classify waste into Organic, Recyclable, Hazardous, and E-waste categories.
• Recommend the appropriate disposal and recycling methods.
• Provide sustainability awareness and environmental guidance.
• Maintain prediction history and analytics for better waste management.
• Support Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 11, SDG 12, and SDG 13).
